How to Calculate Profit Margin and Why It Matters

Date: July 20, 2025

By: Di-Gi

Whether you're selling goods online, running a small shop, or offering services — one number can tell you how healthy your business is: profit margin.

What is Profit Margin?

Profit margin is the percentage of money you keep from each sale after subtracting your costs. It helps you understand if you’re really making money — or just breaking even.

How to Calculate Profit Margin

Use this simple formula:

Profit Margin (%) = ((Selling Price - Cost Price) ÷ Selling Price) × 100

For example, if you buy an item for $20 and sell it for $35:

Profit = $35 - $20 = $15
Profit Margin = ($15 ÷ $35) × 100 = 42.86%

Why Profit Margin Matters

  • Know your real earnings: Sales alone don’t mean success — profits do.
  • Better pricing decisions: Understand how low you can go in discounts or promos.
  • Compare products: See which ones give you better returns.
